Welcome to the hottest club in town… our DJ's will keep the music loud and leave you drained!

It doesn't matter who you are or where you are from, everyone is the same when the music starts and the lights dim low. Club Blood is a place where people from all walks of life can put aside their differences and just be equals for a night. Hidden from the public eye, Club Blood is the only destination that folks are dying to get into.


New management took over the joint, though, with a brand new, bloodless perspective on the idea of having a good time. Why not give these poor, lost souls something even better, a nightlife that goes on and on… for eternity. The new and improved Club Blood really knows how to get the blood flowing. The only requirement to keep this party rockin' is to give all you've got! Blood… soul… no longer needed to keep the party going FOREVER!


As the party has raged, though, so has the demand for the blood and the staff at Club Blood will do whatever is necessary to keep their 'clients' happy. Never has hitting the dance floor been such a draining experience (literally). The undead work endlessly to the music to keep your blood pumping… right out of your body. Enter Club Blood to have the experience of a life(less) time but beware, once you go in, there's no leaving this domain of the night.


Be warned, while Club Blood is the best in nighttime entertainment, don't stray too far from the crowds. There are whispers of hidden chambers within the club that no one ever leaves.
